How Custom Furniture Manufacturers Operate

Custom furniture production is fundamentally different from mass manufacturing. Each order is unique, requiring custom design, engineering, material sourcing, and production planning. Manufacturers must balance flexibility with efficiency, managing complex BOMs, frequent design changes, and tight delivery schedules.

Make-to-Order (MTO) production begins after customer approval, reducing inventory risk but extending lead times. Engineer-to-Order (ETO) includes design and engineering as part of the manufacturing process, offering maximum customization but requiring sophisticated project management.

Revenue models include direct sales, B2B interior fit-out projects, modular systems, and contract manufacturing. Success requires seamless coordination between sales, design, production, quality, and installation teams.
